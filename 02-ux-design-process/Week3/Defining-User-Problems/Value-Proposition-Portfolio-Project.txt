The project is about designing an e-commerce app for an animal shelter. This app will help potential adopters browse detailed information about pets and get matched to those that can fit their lifestyles. Below is a list of the app benefits with the needs of potential users as priority:
- Easy-to-navigate and stunning design.
- User sign up and survey form for personalized experience.
- Suggesting potential matches in an approachable way.
- Providing relevant pet details in a consumable manner.
- Annotated live-streamed video to connect with pets virtually.
- Assisting search through more tools on a bigger screen.
- Easy conversation and interaction with the shelter through chat feature.
- Providing tools to make satisfying decision.
- Post adoption support through forum and chat features.
